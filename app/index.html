World

From treacherous deserts to lands of fire, the world of Sunara contains many distinct landscapes.

Everfrost Mountains

Level Range: 50-60

The harsh winds and snow blankented mountains prove difficult for even the most experienced traveler.

Ensatre Desert

Level Range: 35-45

The hot arid desert of Anali provides little shelter for the weary adventurer.

The Restless Lands

Level Range: 20-25

What was once a lush valley is now a desolate barren wasteland where life is inhospitable.

Ylopmci Forest

Level Range: 1-10

This fog covered northern forest contains numerous natural resources.

Shimmering Tropics

Level Range: 30-45

In the humid jungles of the south lies the ruins of many ancient civilizations.

Swamp of the Crown

Level Range: 15-20

Home to a group of lepers, this treacherous swamp houses many threats.

Forest of the Everliving

Level Range: 45-50

A forest of pure magic, full of wonderous sights.

The Scorched Expanse

Level Range: 55-60

A fiery land full of charred earth and snaking rivers of lava.
